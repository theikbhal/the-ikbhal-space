The Mission

Invisible People is a 501(c)(3) non-profit dedicated to educating the public about homelessness. Founded by Mark Horvath, who himself experienced life on the streets, the platform humanizes a crisis often reduced to statistics.

By using a smartphone and a digital camera, Mark captures the unfiltered reality of those living in tents, cars, and shelters across America.
