African Fertility Rates Ranking

Average number of children per woman (Total Fertility Rate) in African countries. Data reflecting 2024-2025 estimates.

Note: Total Fertility Rate (TFR) represents the average number of children that would be born to a woman over her lifetime if she were to experience the current age-specific fertility rates.

Sources: UN Population Division, World Bank, CIA World Factbook, and national census data (2024-2025 Updates).
